Precompiled Kanye

What is this?

I wanted to create a soundboard of Kanye using sound bytes that I thought best represented an averaged, very mainstream, accessible, but cursory understanding of his character.

The point is that it's really easy to hear these bites and jump to conclusions based on heuristics we've developed over the course of our lives. What's harder is being able to set these notions aside, reevaluating, rederiving, and rebuilding a perspective based on first principles of reason. Instead of hearing, we should try listening--it's through listening that we're able to better understand one another.

As you experience this soundboard, please take a moment to consider aspects of your character and personality that have been groomed and conditioned through your life's experiences, and to what degree each element has been affected. An empirical evaluation isn't necessary, it will do to just acknowledge these various factors.

Enjoy.
